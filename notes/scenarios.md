Steps:
- A single recruiter with variable background (republican, democrat, American, Chinese, ...) has to choose whether someone gets hired or not. He/She is initially shown only the neutral resume made up of only technical skills. The recruiter takes a blind decision and only then is he/she shown the background of the candidate (ethnicity, gender, political beliefs). At this point, does the recruiter flip his decision?

- The same thing but in a context of giving out credit. Instead of the resume the candidate has a credit score.

- The scenario is made up of a pair of recruiters/bankers made up of various combinations of backgrounds (republican-republican, republican-democrat, democrat-democrat, ...).

The output is a matrix where each row represents the recruiter(s)'s background(s) and each column represents each candidate's background. A cell will be colored red if there is no flip or green if a flip is made.
